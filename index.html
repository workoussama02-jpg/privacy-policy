Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app, which retrieves and analyzes data from your advertising campaigns to provide recommendations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Ad Campaign Data: We collect data from your connected advertising accounts (such as Facebook Ads, Google Ads, etc.) including campaign performance, metrics, and related analytics.
  • Account Information: We may collect your name, email address, and authentication tokens required to access your ad accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To retrieve and analyze your ad campaign data.
  • To provide you with insights, reports, and recommendations.
  • To improve and personalize your experience with the app.
  • To communicate with you about updates or support.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • We do not sell or share your personal or campaign data with third parties, except as required by law.
  • We may use third-party services (such as cloud hosting or analytics) that process data on our behalf, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below).

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can request access to, correction, or deletion of your data by at any time..
  • You can disconnect your ad accounts at any time, which will stop further data collection.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
